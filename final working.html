Our Programs

How We Serve Gau Mata

From rescue to rehabilitation, we provide comprehensive care to every cow that comes to our gaushala.

Free Milk Distribution

We distribute fresh milk from our gaushala to saints, ashrams, and charitable hospitals in the region — no commercial sales, only compassion.

Stray Gauvansh Feeding

All stray cows and bulls sheltered in our gaushala are fed with proper green fodder, husk, jaggery, and bran every day.

Rescue & Rehabilitation

We rescue gauvansh from critical conditions and provide shelter, proper nutrition, and full medical facilities until they recover.

Food Distribution

We distribute nutritious food to needy people, underprivileged children, and provide daily necessities to the most vulnerable.

Know Us

Siddha Bal Hanuman Gaurakshan Sansthan

Located in Kekatpur, Amravati, our organization is dedicated to the protection, care, and welfare of desi cows and bulls. We rescue abandoned, injured, and stray gauvansh from the streets and bring them to our gaushala where they are fed, sheltered, and given full medical attention.

Our dedicated team of gausewaks works round the clock to ensure every cow in our care lives a dignified and healthy life. We believe Gau Seva is not just a religious duty — it is a commitment to compassion and community.

What We Do

Our Core Activities

🛡️

Cow Protection

We bring abandoned and stray desi cows and bulls to our gaushala and give them a safe, permanent home.

🏠

Shelter

Spacious, well-ventilated cow sheds ensure every gauvansh lives comfortably with dignity and care.

💊

Medical Facility

Qualified veterinarians available 24×7, with in-house medicines, vaccinations, and emergency care.

🌿

Organic Farming

We promote pesticide-free farming and use cow dung to produce organic fertilizers for local farmers.

🔥

Yagya & Puja

Sacred rituals, Yagyas, and Poojas conducted by experienced Vedic Brahmins for wellbeing of all.

🤝

Community Help

We support affected communities during calamities by providing food, medicines, and shelter.

♻️

Bio Fertilizer Unit

Converting cow dung into organic manure and promoting eco-friendly agriculture in Maharashtra.

☀️

Renewable Energy

Solar panels and biogas plants power our gaushala, reducing our carbon footprint sustainably.
